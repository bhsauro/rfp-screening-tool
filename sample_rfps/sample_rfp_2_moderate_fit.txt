REQUEST FOR PROPOSAL
Federal Data Integration and Analytics Platform

Issuing Organization: U.S. Department of Transportation - Federal Transit Administration
RFP Number: FTA-2026-IT-0847
Issue Date: April 20, 2026
Submission Deadline: May 25, 2026 (2:00 PM EST)

1. BACKGROUND

The Federal Transit Administration (FTA) manages grant programs and oversight for public transportation across the United States. We are seeking a qualified consulting firm to modernize our data integration infrastructure and build a comprehensive analytics platform to consolidate reporting from over 1,500 transit agencies nationwide.

2. PROJECT OVERVIEW

The selected vendor will design and implement a cloud-based data integration platform that:

- Consolidates data from 1,500+ transit agencies using varied reporting formats
- Integrates with existing federal grant management systems
- Develops real-time dashboards and analytics for program oversight
- Ensures FedRAMP compliance and data security requirements
- Provides automated data validation and quality control workflows
- Creates public-facing transparency portal for grant allocations and performance metrics

3. SCOPE OF WORK

Phase 1 (Months 1-4): Discovery and Architecture
- Stakeholder engagement across regional offices
- Data mapping and integration architecture design
- Security and compliance framework development

Phase 2 (Months 5-9): Platform Development
- Cloud infrastructure setup (AWS GovCloud required)
- ETL pipeline development
- Dashboard and reporting tool implementation
- API development for agency data submissions

Phase 3 (Months 10-12): Testing and Deployment
- User acceptance testing across all FTA offices
- Staff training and change management
- Phased rollout to transit agencies
- Documentation and knowledge transfer

Total Duration: 12 months

4. TECHNICAL REQUIREMENTS

Required capabilities:
- AWS GovCloud expertise and implementation experience
- Experience with large-scale ETL and data integration (100M+ records)
- Business intelligence and visualization platforms (Tableau, Power BI, or similar)
- API development and documentation
- FedRAMP compliance experience (preferred but not required)
- Understanding of federal grant management processes

5. ELIGIBILITY REQUIREMENTS

Proposers must:
- Minimum 5 years experience with federal or state government clients
- Experience with data integration projects serving 500+ data sources
- AWS certification (Solutions Architect or equivalent)
- Ability to pass background checks for team members requiring facility access
- Professional liability insurance ($3M minimum)
- U.S.-based company with physical presence on East Coast

Note: Active FedRAMP authorization is highly preferred but not mandatory. Firms without FedRAMP experience must demonstrate clear path to compliance.

6. PROJECT TIMELINE

AGGRESSIVE TIMELINE: Due to Congressional reporting requirements, the platform must be operational by April 2027. This timeline is firm and non-negotiable.

Proposal deadline: May 25, 2026 (3 weeks to prepare)
Award date: June 15, 2026
Project start: July 1, 2026
Go-live: April 1, 2027

7. BUDGET

Estimated budget: $800,000 - $1,200,000

Budget includes all consulting services, cloud infrastructure costs, software licensing, training, and 6 months post-deployment support.

8. EVALUATION CRITERIA

Proposals will be evaluated on:
- Federal government experience and understanding of compliance requirements (30%)
- Technical approach and architecture (25%)
- Data integration and analytics expertise (20%)
- Team qualifications and experience (15%)
- Cost and value (10%)

9. SPECIAL CONSIDERATIONS

Challenges and constraints:
- Short proposal preparation window (3 weeks)
- Aggressive 9-month implementation timeline
- Complex stakeholder landscape (50 state DOTs, 10 regional offices, 1,500 agencies)
- FedRAMP compliance pathway required (can be developed during project if not currently certified)
- May require periodic travel to Washington, DC and regional offices
- Background checks required for staff accessing federal systems (4-6 week process)

Strengths of opportunity:
- Aligns with workflow automation and data integration expertise
- Federal client in target public sector vertical
- East Coast location (DC area)
- Clear scope and deliverables
- Potential for follow-on work (Phase 2: ML/AI analytics enhancements)
