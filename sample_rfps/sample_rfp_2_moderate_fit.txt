REQUEST FOR PROPOSAL
Enterprise Resource Planning System Implementation

Issuing Organization: Mountain View Regional Hospital System
RFP Number: MVRHS-IT-2026-089
Issue Date: April 25, 2026
Submission Deadline: May 15, 2026 (3:00 PM EST)

1. BACKGROUND

Mountain View Regional Hospital System operates 4 hospitals and 12 outpatient clinics across three states. We are seeking an experienced consulting firm to implement a comprehensive Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) system to replace our legacy financial, HR, and supply chain management systems.

2. PROJECT SCOPE

The selected vendor will be responsible for:

- Full ERP system selection and implementation
- Data migration from legacy systems (multiple platforms, some dating to 1990s)
- Integration with existing Electronic Health Record (EHR) system
- Custom module development for healthcare-specific workflows
- Multi-site deployment and coordination
- Staff training across all facilities
- Post-implementation support (12 months)

3. TECHNICAL REQUIREMENTS

- Must support multi-entity consolidation
- HIPAA compliance mandatory
- Integration with Epic EHR system
- Support for complex healthcare billing workflows
- Real-time financial reporting and analytics
- Mobile accessibility for supply chain management

4. PROJECT TIMELINE

URGENT: The hospital system requires implementation to be completed by December 31, 2026 to comply with new regulatory requirements. This is a firm deadline.

Proposed schedule:
- Award Date: May 30, 2026
- Implementation Start: June 5, 2026
- Go-Live Target: December 15, 2026
- Total Duration: 6.5 months

5. ELIGIBILITY REQUIREMENTS

Vendors must:
- Be certified implementation partners for at least one major ERP platform (SAP, Oracle, Workday, or similar)
- Have completed minimum of 2 healthcare ERP implementations
- Maintain active HIPAA compliance certification
- Provide 24/7 support capability
- Have minimum annual revenue of $50M
- Carry E&O insurance of at least $5M

Note: Preference will be given to firms with previous multi-state healthcare system experience.

6. BUDGET

Budget range: $1.2M - $2.5M (inclusive of software licensing, implementation, training, and first year support)

Budget constraints are strict. Proposals exceeding $2.5M will not be considered.

7. PROPOSAL REQUIREMENTS

Submit the following by May 15, 2026:

- Executive summary
- Company qualifications and certifications
- Proposed ERP platform with justification
- Detailed implementation methodology
- Risk mitigation strategies for aggressive timeline
- Project team composition (must include healthcare ERP specialists)
- Detailed cost breakdown
- Minimum of 5 references from healthcare implementations completed in last 3 years

8. EVALUATION

Proposals will be scored as follows:
- Technical approach and platform selection (25%)
- Healthcare industry experience (25%)
- Timeline feasibility and risk mitigation (20%)
- Team qualifications (15%)
- Cost (15%)

9. SPECIAL CONSIDERATIONS

- The selected vendor must begin work immediately upon contract execution
- On-site presence required at main hospital campus during implementation
- Integration with Epic EHR is critical and non-negotiable
- Union considerations: Hospital staff are unionized; any changes to workflows must be coordinated with labor representatives
