REQUEST FOR PROPOSAL
Manufacturing Equipment Installation and Process Engineering Services

Issuing Organization: Apex Automotive Components Inc.
RFP Number: AAC-MANUF-2026-0447
Issue Date: April 28, 2026
Submission Deadline: May 8, 2026 (12:00 PM CST)

1. PROJECT SUMMARY

Apex Automotive Components Inc. is a Tier 1 automotive parts supplier specializing in precision-machined transmission components. We are seeking proposals from qualified engineering firms to design, procure, install, and commission a new CNC machining center production line at our Dearborn, Michigan facility.

2. SCOPE OF WORK

The selected firm will provide:

- Industrial engineering services for production line layout and workflow optimization
- Specification and procurement of CNC machining equipment (5-axis mills, turning centers, automated material handling)
- Electrical and mechanical installation oversight
- PLC programming and SCADA system integration
- Robotic cell programming and commissioning
- ISO/TS 16949 compliance documentation
- Operator training on new equipment
- Production validation and run-off procedures per automotive industry standards

3. TECHNICAL SPECIFICATIONS

Equipment Requirements:
- (5) Mazak 5-axis horizontal machining centers
- (3) Okuma turning centers with sub-spindle and live tooling
- Automated pallet pool system with 24-pallet capacity
- Fanuc robotic loading/unloading cells
- Coolant management and chip removal systems
- Integration with existing MES (Manufacturing Execution System)

Quality Requirements:
- SPC (Statistical Process Control) integration
- CMM (Coordinate Measuring Machine) programming for in-process inspection
- PPAP (Production Part Approval Process) documentation
- FMEA (Failure Mode Effects Analysis) for new processes

4. PROJECT TIMELINE

CRITICAL: Production line must be operational by August 15, 2026 to meet OEM contract obligations. Contract includes liquidated damages of $50,000 per week for delays.

- Award: May 12, 2026
- Equipment procurement: May 15 - June 30, 2026
- Installation: July 1 - August 1, 2026
- Commissioning and validation: August 1 - August 15, 2026
- Total timeline: 10 weeks from award

5. MANDATORY QUALIFICATIONS

Proposers MUST have:
- Minimum 10 years of automotive manufacturing engineering experience
- Professional Engineer (PE) license in the state of Michigan
- IATF 16949 Lead Auditor certification
- Prior experience with Fanuc CNC controls and robotic systems
- Demonstrated experience with at least 3 automotive Tier 1 production line installations
- ISO 9001 and IATF 16949 certified quality management systems
- Specific experience with transmission component manufacturing
- On-staff process engineers with Six Sigma Black Belt certification
- 24/7 on-call support capability during commissioning

Additional Requirements:
- Must be approved vendor with GM, Ford, or Stellantis
- Must carry $10M general liability and $5M professional liability insurance
- OSHA 30-hour certification required for all on-site personnel
- Must pass facility background checks and NDA requirements

6. BUDGET

Payment terms: Net 90 days after milestone completion. No advance payments will be made.
