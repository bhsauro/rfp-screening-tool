REQUEST FOR PROPOSAL
Digital Transformation Consulting Services

Issuing Organization: City of Portland Public Works Department
RFP Number: PW-2026-0142
Issue Date: April 15, 2026
Submission Deadline: June 1, 2026 (5:00 PM PST)

1. PROJECT OVERVIEW

The City of Portland Public Works Department is seeking proposals from qualified consulting firms to provide digital transformation consulting services for our infrastructure management systems. The selected vendor will help modernize our asset management, work order systems, and public-facing service portals.

2. SCOPE OF WORK

The consulting firm will be expected to:

- Conduct a comprehensive assessment of current infrastructure management systems
- Develop a digital transformation roadmap aligned with city priorities
- Design and implement modern asset management solutions
- Create citizen-facing digital service portals
- Provide change management and staff training
- Ensure compliance with municipal data security requirements

3. PROJECT TIMELINE

- Project Kickoff: July 1, 2026
- Assessment Phase: 2 months
- Design Phase: 3 months
- Implementation: 6 months
- Total Duration: 12 months

4. ELIGIBILITY REQUIREMENTS

Proposers must:
- Have a minimum of 5 years of experience in public sector digital transformation
- Demonstrate experience with at least 3 municipal infrastructure projects
- Possess current certifications in relevant technologies (cloud platforms, cybersecurity)
- Provide proof of professional liability insurance ($2M minimum)
- Be registered to do business in Oregon

5. BUDGET

The estimated budget for this project is $450,000 - $650,000, subject to negotiation based on proposed scope and deliverables.

6. EVALUATION CRITERIA

Proposals will be evaluated on:
- Relevant experience and qualifications (35%)
- Technical approach and methodology (30%)
- Project team expertise (20%)
- Cost competitiveness (15%)

7. SUBMISSION REQUIREMENTS

Interested firms must submit:
- Company profile and qualifications
- Detailed technical proposal
- Project timeline and work plan
- Team resumes and organizational chart
- Cost proposal with itemized breakdown
- Three references from similar projects
